What you need to know about Malware

Malware is on the rise for mobile devices. Increased reliance smartphones has also increased the market for mobile malware.

You risk having your information stolen, corrupted, or held for ransom. This includes information such as:

  • Online banking accounts
  • Social media profiles
  • Personal files such as pictures or important downloads
